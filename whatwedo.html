What We Do

NextRecruit is a Recruiting and B2B Sales lead platform that can be used however you want depending on what your personal or business goals are. The platform provides users with contact information from over 5000+ hockey teams around the world. The contact information ranges from Head coach, Assistant Coach, Goalie Coach, Director of Recruiting, Owner, President, Marketing directors, Tickets Sales, Broadcast, HR, Sales, Corporate Sales and more.

Leagues We Cover

Players or companies often target a precise team or league that they want to get in touch with. For this reason, NextRecruit has over 10,000+ contact information from over 100 leagues around the world. In Canada, we have contact information from organizations in the BCHL, AJHL, SJHL, MJHL, OJHL, NOJHL, CCHL, QJHL, MHL, Prep Schools, CSSHL, Usport Men's, Usport Womens and more. In the United States, we cover NCAA I, NCAA I W, NCAA III, NCAA III W, USHL, NAHL, Prep Schools, SPHL, FHL, ECHL and more. In Europe we cover all of the high level leagues across the continent which allows users to get in touch with pretty much anyone in the industry.

How Does the platform Work

Using a credit-based system, users are able to use their credits to access the dials and email address of professionals. By doing so, users get direct contact with decision-makers of every single hockey organization. Contacts information are sometimes worth more than 1 credit because those are considered “High end” professionals. This strategy helps coaches only receive serious emails from users on the NextRecruit platform. It is a strategy that has been adopted in order to give coaches and users the best experience possible.

How do I Contact a Coach/Professional?

NextRecruit recommends to firstly identify which organization/league you want to get in touch with. Using filter features, you will be able to clearly identify those prospects. Once you see the prospects from your potential organization, we recommend identifying the professional you want to reach out to based on your personal goal. Once you identify which profile you want information from, use your credits to access their dial and email address. We recommend to initially write an introduction email to present yourself. We have many templates available that are industry-proven to work and give user a better chance to get a reply. From there, it is up to the prospect and the user to entertain the exchange.
